PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: 2026-03-06 // PROTOCOL: SEC-01

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you join the Drill Protocol waitlist or create an account, we collect your name, email address, and platform preference. During app usage, we collect drill completion data, scores, and performance metrics to improve your training experience.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used to: provide and improve the Drill Protocol service, send you notifications during active drills, maintain leaderboards and scoring, and communicate product updates. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored on encrypted servers. Drill performance data is anonymized for aggregate analytics. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting base@svgnsystems.com.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Drill Protocol uses third-party services for form processing (Formspree), analytics, and push notifications. These services have their own privacy policies. We minimize the data shared with third parties to what is strictly necessary for functionality.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also object to processing or request data portability. Contact base@svgnsystems.com for any privacy-related requests.
